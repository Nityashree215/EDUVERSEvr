# 🌐 Eduverse VR – Virtual Reality-Based Learning Platform

**Eduverse VR** is an innovative Virtual Reality (VR)-based educational platform developed to provide **quality, immersive, and accessible learning experiences** for students in **rural and underprivileged areas**. By simulating realistic classrooms, science labs, libraries, and creative zones, Eduverse VR helps bridge the educational divide between urban and rural regions.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🎓 **Virtual Classrooms**  
  - Live & recorded lectures with interactive VR blackboards

- 🔬 **Science Laboratories (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)**  
  - Safe virtual experiments with animations and tools

- 📚 **E-Library & E-Books**  
  - Multi-language support, audio narration, offline mode

- 📊 **Academic Performance Tracker**  
  - Subject-wise reports, attendance, progress visualization

- 🧠 **Skill Development Arena**  
  - Coding, language learning, AI, robotics with certifications

- 🎨 **Personal Interest Zone**  
  - Drawing, craft designing, handwork activities in 3D

- 🗣️ **Communication & Collaboration**  
  - Real-time voice chat, doubt-solving rooms, group discussion

---

## 🏗️ System Architecture

- **Front-End (VR Interface):** Unity 3D, Blender  
- **Back-End:** Python (Flask) or Node.js  
- **Database:** Firebase / MongoDB  
- **Cloud Storage:** AWS or Google Cloud  
- **AI & Accessibility:** Google TTS, Speech Recognition  
- **Web Dashboard:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript

---

## 💡 Objectives

- Deliver **interactive education to rural students**.
- Provide access to **virtual labs, classrooms, and e-resources**.
- Encourage **skill building and creativity** beyond the classroom.
- Support **teachers** with performance insights and communication tools.
