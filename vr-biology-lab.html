Experiment Results: Mitosis Stages

You successfully observed the stages of mitosis:

  1. Prophase: Chromosomes become visible, spindle fibers appear.
  2. Metaphase: Chromosomes align at the cell's equator.
  3. Anaphase: Chromosomes separate and move toward opposite poles.
  4. Telophase: Two nuclei form; cell division begins.
